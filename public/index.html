Why Accompany

Our Purpose | The Ikigai of Accompany

Because nobody should miss out on life, for lack of company!

At Accompany, we believe growing older should mean growing richer - richer in terms of experiences, conversations, and joy.

But very often, the simple logistics of stepping out keep our elders from living their lives fully and fulfillingly. It could either be moving around the city or going on a spiritual trip or a destination of their choice. Imagine dreaming a tirth yatra like Kashi Rameshwaram for ages, doctor’s visit postponed, a sunrise by the beach missed, a music kutcheri or a movie or a dance drama skipped because there’s no support system - literally, no one to go with!

That's Accompany - a company in need; indeed ❤.

We’re not caregivers. We’re neither a cab service. We are companions.

(Thoughtful, Trustworthy and Present)

  • "The friend who says ‘I’ll come with you.’"
  • "The daughter who holds your hand on a temple step."
  • "The grandson who waits patiently while you finish shopping."
🌟

Restore independence through companionship.

Our Ikigai^ is simple. Ikigai in Japanese means ‘a reason for living’.

‘a reason for giving’.

What We Do

Our Service

Accompany provides trusted companions for senior citizens across communities, families, and social circles. Think of us as your extended family member, available when you need someone by your side.

In the last 5 years, we’ve travelled alongside and accompanied a wide cross-section of people on their pilgrimage journeys across ‘Incredible India’, temple and hospital visits, and walks in the park.

🛫

Travel

1:1 Accompany Yatra (Sr. Citizens)

Designed specifically for senior citizens, to support families who want 1:1 care for parents or grandparents on their tirth yatras or vacay to any other destinations. Curated for senior citizens, who love to travel on their dates, at their pace, with undivided attention.

  • 1. Totally Customized : You choose the place, dates, and duration. Kashi or your Kuladeivam, Mathura or Mantralayam. Want only one temple per day? Done.
  • 2. Dedicated Sakha / Sakhi : One companion (1:1) travels along from home to home. Allow them to manage everything - tickets, hotel, food, dharshan queues, meds, wheelchair, and a helping hand.
  • 3. Comfort First : Senior-friendly hotels, ground floor rooms, healthy diet, and extra rest days. Your comfort and health guides the itinerary.

Group Accompany Yatra (Open-2-All)

Curated Group Trips, open to all ages - solo travelers, friends and families.

Our Heritage

The Philosophy of Sakha & Sakhi

Deeply rooted in Indian heritage, trust, and selfless companionship.

The Sakha

The Guide, The Friend, The Presence

In the epic Mahabharata, Lord Krishna acts as the Sakha (friend, guide, and charioteer) to Arjuna. Krishna does not fight the war himself; instead, he holds the reins of the horses, stands by Arjuna in moments of deep doubt, and guides him through every storm.

This is the essence of our Sakha service for elderly men. We do not take away our elders' independence; we hold the reins, guide them through the crowded temple steps, hospital visits, and travel, standing by them as trusted companions. We are simply present, deeply supportive, and empowering.

The Sakhi

The Protective, Trustworthy Friend

Draupadi's relationship with Krishna represents the ultimate bond of a Sakhi—a trustworthy, absolute support system. In her most challenging hour of need, when no one else stood by her, her Sakha, Krishna, was present instantly, offering infinite protection and restoring her dignity and strength.

This is the essence of our Sakhi service for elderly women and parents whose children are far away. A Sakhi is a daughter-like presence, holding their hand, protecting their comfort, and ensuring they are never alone in times of need. Her presence is a shield against loneliness and logistical hurdles.

🤝 Conduct & Character

Accompany has ‘Zero Tolerance’ for misconduct. By anyone. On either side.

A. If you feel unsafe or uncomfortable with your Sakha / Sakhi:

Your safety and dignity are non-negotiable. If a Sakha / Sakhi engages in unsolicited touch, dirty talk, disrespect, financial requests, or any behavior that makes you uncomfortable:

  1. Stop the service immediately. You are never obligated to continue.
  2. Call our 24x7 helpline at +91-XXXXXXXXXX. Ask for “Urgent Conduct Issue.”
  3. We act within 1 hour: We will remove the Sakha / Sakhi, ensure you are safe, and arrange alternate support if needed.
  4. Investigation & Action: Every complaint is investigated. Verified misconduct leads to immediate termination, police complaint if applicable, and blacklisting from Accompany forever.
B. If a Sakha / Sakhi faces misconduct from a senior citizen or a family member:

Don't you agree? Our Sakhas / Sakhis deserve the same respect and safety. If they face verbal abuse, unsolicited touch, inappropriate requests, or harassment:

  1. They are instructed to exit immediately and report to us.
  2. We investigate within 24 hours. Verified misconduct by a senior citizen or a family member leads to immediate termination of service, no refunds, and blacklisting from Accompany.
  3. We protect our people. No Sakha / Sakhi will be sent back ever, to an unsafe environment.
Our Safeguards:
  • 📌 1. Code of Conduct: Every Sakha / Sakhi signs and is trained on Sakha Dharma + strict conduct code before their first assignment.
  • 📌 2. Background Checks: Due diligence and reference checks combined with ID verification for all Sakhas and Sakhis.
  • 📌 3. Live Check-ins: For 1:1 services, Sakhas and Sakhis check in with our team at start, mid-point, and end.
  • 📌 4. Family Loop: For senior services, we keep one family member on WhatsApp updates.

Please visit our page on Sakha Sakhi Ethos. The foundation of Sakha Sakhi Dharma is built on Maryada i.e. Dignity. We will fiercely protect it - for our senior citizens of course, but equally for our Sakhas and Sakhis.
